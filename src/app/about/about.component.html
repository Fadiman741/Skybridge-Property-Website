Rentals

We provide a comprehensive letting service to landlords and owners of sectional title properties. Our distinctive and concentrated method guarantees that we locate the most suitable tenants for your property. SkyBridge employs a committed team of letting agents who strive to meet specific goals each month. We specialize in the letting of residential, commercial, and retail properties. Furthermore, our exclusive student portal, extensive knowledge of student housing requirements in South Africa, and strong connections with the country's major higher education institutions make us the preferred option for students seeking ideal accommodations.

Rental management

At SkyBridge, we have a deep understanding of the requirements of landlords. Our advanced software, combined with our extensive knowledge and skills, enables us to provide a comprehensive rental management solution. Our top priority is to ensure that you receive timely and complete rental payments, and that all levies, utilities, and rates are regularly updated. We also strive to maintain your property in excellent condition. Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our emphasis on using recycled buildings across South Africa. In addition to carrying out standard property management tasks, we aim to enhance the areas surrounding your property by collaborating closely with central improvement districts, city councils, and other organizations that share our values.

Body corporate management

We have developed a comprehensive solution for body corporate management, which has become one of our core strengths in recent years. By collaborating closely with body corporates, we ensure efficient control, management, maintenance, and administration of properties nationwide. Our services include building repairs and maintenance, financial budgeting and reporting, contract management, enforcing rules and by-laws, addressing owner concerns and resolving disputes, collecting levies and overdue payments, arranging meetings, and issuing notices, orders, and certificates. At Skybridge, we aim to alleviate the burden, stress, and pressure of body corporate roles and responsibilities by managing them seamlessly and precisely.
